Who are we?

With a family of 6, we strive on the smiles on people's faces with our hotdogs and other filipino-based cuisine! From a small idea from Ness Alemania, Co-Owner of "Big Bad Bob's Hotodgs", it grew into a massive business where every day is a new opportunity to make a person's day a little bit better. From your simple hotdogs, to bridging out and trying new things like our Filipino Siomai, or our Filipino-style Barbeque sticks. We remind our community that our sole purpose of managing our wholesome business is to introduce our traditions with various kinds of foods and put smiles on people's faces! 😁
